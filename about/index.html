About

LS-DYNA Material Hub is a lightweight website for browsing LS-DYNA material models and generating ready-to-use keyword cards.

What you can do

  • Search material models by MAT number, name, tags, and keywords.
  • Open a model page with key parameters and references.
  • Use the Keyword Generator to export a ready-to-use .k file.

Scope

  • Static site (GitHub Pages friendly).
  • No login, no backend.
  • Data-driven via data/materials.json and data/templates.json.
