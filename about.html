About

LS-DYNA Material Hub is a small project to help users quickly find LS-DYNA material models and define keywords using (1) short theory notes, (2) runnable keyword samples, and (3) parameterized generators.

  • Library: searchable list of models
  • Details: theory + workflow + update history
  • Generator: edit inputs (with defaults) and export keyword
